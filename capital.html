CAPITAL


A stylish café based in the heart of New York’st historic Financial District.

  • Client Requirements


    - Create a home page for CAPITAL.

    - The Home Page must have a “Our Values” section and a call to action to get customers to subscribe to their newsletter.

    - In total, there must be a hero section with two other sections and a footer.

    Skip to Final Design


  • Brand Identity Keywords


    Luxury, Sophisticated, Elegant, Refined, Professional, Upscale, Timeless, Classy, Stylish, Chic, Tailored, Metropolitan


  • Brand Mission


    CAPITAL is a one stop shop for commuters seeking refuge from busy lunch times and morning rush.


  • Duration | Role | Team | Tools


    One Week, UI/UX Designer, Independent, Figma


  • Research Questions


    - What makes a brand luxurious?

    - Who is the ideal customer?

    - How can I convey the message of refuge from the chaos of work through visual design alone?

    - How do other coffee shop websites in the area compare to CAPITAL’s website and how do they convey their brand?


  • User Stories


    "As a financial analyst in New York's bustling Financial District, my days are incredibly hectic and stressful. I need a serene coffee shop close to my office where I can escape the noise and chaos, even if just for a few moments. ”

    "As a corporate lawyer with a demanding job in the Financial District, I need a sophisticated coffee shop nearby where I can enjoy top-quality coffee and unwind. This place offers me a moment of tranquility amidst my busy schedule. "


  • Competitive Analysis



    Blue Bottle Coffee


    Does not have brand logo clearly displayed on hero section, more emphasis on products than brand. Did not like the idea of selling a product immediately rather than introducing the brand.


    SUITED


    Strong emphasis on Financial District community influence, specifically the people who work in the Financial District. The identity is conveyed through pictures and phrases such as “tailor made” and pictures of people in suits.


    Black Fox


    Sleek and modern design. Does not feel as if you are being sold a product, rather it is an experience to visit Black Fox. Visually conveys the impression of serenity away from the rush of the Financial District.



    Key Takeaways

    - You are not selling a product you are selling an experience through your

    - Dark Themes convey a luxury design in relation to New York.

    - Consistently adhere to brand identity and mission through home page.
