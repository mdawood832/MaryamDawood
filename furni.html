Furni


A furniture store that offers beautiful, functional furniture at various prices with flexible financing to suit any budget.

  • Overview



    Design a user-friendly, aesthetically pleasing website/app for Furni, ensuring an excellent customer experience from browsing to purchasing furniture with flexible financing options.


    Skip to Final Design

    Duration

    2 Weeks

    Role

    UX Research, UI Design

    Team

    Independent

    Tools

    Figma, Relume

  • Project Goals


    - Enhance User Experience: Create an intuitive and engaging user interface that simplifies the browsing and purchasing process.

    - Highlight Financing Options: Clearly present financing plans and make them easily accessible and understandable to all users.

    - Boost Conversion Rates: Design with a focus on increasing sales and reducing cart abandonment.

    - Brand Identity: Reflect Furni's friendly and approachable brand personality in the design.


  • User Personas


    Young Couple Emma and Lucas

    Age: 28 and 30

    Occupation: Marketing Manager and Software Developer

    Location: Urban Area, Apartment Living

    Income: Combined $120,000/year



    Goals
    - Create a stylish and comfortable living space to relax and entertain friends.

    - Invest in high-quality, functional furniture that will last as they plan to buy a house in the future.

    - Take advantage of financing options to spread out the cost without straining their monthly budget.

    Challenges
    - Balancing aesthetic preferences and functionality within their apartment space.

    - Managing student loan payments alongside new furniture purchases.

    - Ensuring the furniture fits the modern, sleek style they prefer.



    Retired Empty Nesters Linda and Robert

    Age: 65 and 67

    Occupation: Retired Nurse and Engineer

    Location: Rural Area, Cozy Cottage

    Income: Combined $70,000/year (pension and savings)



    Goals
    - Redecorate their home to reflect their current lifestyle and personal tastes.

    Challenges
    - Ensuring furniture is comfortable and easy to use as they get older.

  • Pain Points


    - Difficulty balancing quality and affordability.

    - Challenges in managing finances with large furniture purchases.

    - Need for durable, functional furniture that suits their lifestyle.


  • Problem Statement


    How might we create a seamless and friendly shopping experience that conveys flexible financing options and emphasis on quality, durability, and functionality?


  • Competitive Analysis



    Wayfair


    Wayfair prominently features flexible financing options, making it accessible for a wide range of customers. These options are clearly displayed, allowing users to understand and take advantage of payment plans that suit their financial needs

    Provides detailed product descriptions, customer reviews, and ratings. This transparency helps emphasize the quality and durability of the items, allowing customers to make informed purchasing decisions.

    Living Spaces


    Has a banner for financing that links to the financing page.

    Living Spaces clearly presents its financing options, allowing customers to choose payment plans that fit their budget. These options are prominently displayed and easily accessible, helping to convey the flexibility and affordability of their offerings.

    The website emphasizes the quality and durability of its products by providing detailed descriptions, high-resolution images, and comprehensive customer reviews. This transparency helps build trust and assures customers of the long-lasting value of their purchases.



    Medley


    The website features a harmonious blend of modern and classic design elements, creating an inviting and elegant atmosphere. The user interface is clean and uncluttered, with a soothing color palette of warm neutrals and soft pastels that exudes comfort and sophistication.

    Medley’s financing options are not as clearly displayed as its competitors. The financing page is not found at the top of the page rather it is at the bottom tucked away in the footer.

    The clean design and intuitive navigation make for an enjoyable and efficient browsing experience.
