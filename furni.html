Furni


A furniture store offering beautiful, functional pieces at a range of prices, with flexible financing options to fit any budget.

  • Overview


    Design a user-friendly, visually appealing website for Furni, providing a seamless user experience from browsing to purchasing, with flexible financing options.


    Duration

    6 Weeks

    Role

    UX Research, UI Design

    Team

    Independent

    Tools

    Figma, Relume

  • Final Design


  • Research


    I interviewed 5 people currently searching for new furniture to validate my initial hypothesis and explore their pain points. The semi-structured interviews revealed challenges users face when navigating online furniture shopping.


  • User Personas



  • Customer Pain Points


    - Difficulty balancing quality and affordability.

    - Challenges in managing finances with large furniture purchases.

    - Need for durable, functional furniture that suits their lifestyle.


  • Problem Statement


    How might we design a seamless and user-friendly shopping experience that highlights flexible financing options and emphasizes quality, durability, and functionality?


  • Competitive Analysis



    Wayfair


    Wayfair prominently features flexible financing options, making it accessible for customers.

    Provides detailed product descriptions, customer reviews, and ratings.

    Living Spaces


    Has a banner for financing that links to the financing page.

    The website emphasizes the quality and durability of its products by providing detailed descriptions, high-resolution images, and comprehensive customer reviews.



    Medley


    modern and classic design elements with an elegant experience. Soothing color palette of warm neutrals and soft pastels.

    Medley’s financing options are not as clearly displayed as its competitors. The financing page is not found at the top of the page rather it is at the bottom tucked away in the footer.



  • Project Goals

    - Enhance User Experience: Create an intuitive and engaging user interface that simplifies the browsing and purchasing process.

    - Highlight Financing Options: Clearly present financing plans and make them easily accessible and understandable to all users.

    - Brand Identity: Reflect Furni's friendly and approachable brand personality in the design.

    - Durability: Emphasize the quality and durability of Furni's products.

  • Features/Final Design

    Home Page



    Emphasizing financing through the navigation, financing section, and footer too meet user's pain points.

    Collections Page



    Allow customers to filter by furniture type, date, price, and ratings. Each product should display a clear image and price. Sale items are marked with an icon and highlighted in red, while out-of-stock items have a banner.

    Product Details Page



    This page features comprehensive product information, including a carousel of images and detailed descriptions to highlight furniture quality. It provides ratings, assembly instructions, warranty details, and shipping information. An Affirm icon prominently displays financing options to emphasize flexibility.

    Clearance Page



    This page features all sale items, specifically designed for those seeking budget-friendly options.

    Financing Page



    The page provides detailed information on Furni's various financing options. It covers essential details customers need and is accessible via the navigation bar, footer, and home page.

  • Checkout Process


    Designed a checkout experience that enables users to pay using Affirm financing.
