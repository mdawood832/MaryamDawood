  • Overview


    As an avid follower of Joe Dispenza’s teachings, I often struggled to connect his transformative methods to my daily challenges. To bridge this gap, I began training an AI (ChatGPT) with his principles, allowing me to receive personalized guidance in real time. This experience inspired me to propose a scalable AI-powered platform to the Head of Creative at Unlimited, aimed at making Joe Dispenza’s teachings more accessible and actionable for a global audience.

    Joe Dispenza is a neuroscientist, author, and speaker known for his work on the mind-body connection. He teaches how meditation and thought patterns can rewire the brain, enabling personal transformation, healing, and creating lasting change. His work continues to inspire people worldwide, especially through his books, meditations, and retreats.
